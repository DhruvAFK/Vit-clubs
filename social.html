Social Welfare and Development Club

The SW&D Committee aims to empower society through social work, volunteering, and awareness programs, following the motto 'Help One, Inspire Many'.The Social Welfare & Development Committee (SW&D) at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, is a student organization dedicated to fostering sustainable social change through various community service initiatives. The committee operates under the motto "Help One, Inspire Many" and aims to empower society by organizing events throughout the year that promote social responsibility and civic engagement.
