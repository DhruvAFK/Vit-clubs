Robotics Club — The Robotics Forum (TRF)

The Robotics Forum is VIT Pune’s robotics organization with a 17-year legacy. Students collaborate on automation and innovation projects, participating in national events like Robocon and IIT Techfest. The club has a strong track record, having participated in ABU Robocon since 2007 and consistently performing among the top teams, with notable achievements including a 2nd place in South India in 2017 and a 3rd place in the South India zone in 2018. TRF provides students with hands-on experience in sensors, motors, embedded C, microcontrollers, computer vision, and app development through workshops and industrial or open-source projects.
